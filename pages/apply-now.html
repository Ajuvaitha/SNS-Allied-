Begin Your Journey in Allied Health Science

Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Students must submit the admission enquiry form with basic details including name, contact information, and preferred course.

2

Eligibility Verification

The admissions team verifies academic eligibility based on 10+2 qualification with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB).

3

Counselling / Interaction

Applicants may be invited for counselling or interaction to understand course options and career pathways in Allied Health Science.

4

Admission Confirmation

Selected candidates receive admission confirmation after document verification and payment of the required fees.
